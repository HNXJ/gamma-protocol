# Citation and Evidence Policy

All scientific claims in Gamma must be attributed and evidenced.

## Citation Style
Use standard academic citations where possible.
- **Papers:** DOI and OpenAlex ID preferred.
- **Datasets:** DOI or stable URI.

## Evidence Mappings
Every mapping from a source to a model parameter must include:
- The exact quote or figure reference.
- The agent that performed the extraction.
- The confidence rating assigned by the judge.
