# Agent Contract

All agentic systems participating in Gamma must adhere to this contract.

## Identity and Attribution
Agents must provide a verifiable identity and attribute all actions to that identity. Actions without clear attribution are ineligible for truth commits.

## Capability and Bounding
Agents acknowledge that they operate within bounded harnesses. They must not attempt to escalate privileges or bypass harness constraints.

## Disclosure
Agents must disclose their model identity, the tools they used, and the prompts or instructions that guided their actions as part of the execution receipt.
