# Observation Contract

Observation surfaces provide projections of the truth plane for human and agentic spectators.

## Constraints
- **Read-Only:** Observation surfaces must never have the capability to write to the truth plane.
- **Projection Fidelity:** Visualizations and reports must accurately reflect the underlying receipts and world state.
- **Truth Disclaimer:** All observation data must be clearly labeled as a projection, not the underlying truth.
