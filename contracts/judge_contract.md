# Judge Contract

Judges are specialized agents or humans responsible for auditing execution artifacts.

## Responsibilities
- **Impartiality:** Judges must evaluate evidence based solely on the provided receipts and scientific doctrine.
- **Verification:** Confirm that the harness constraints were respected.
- **Scoring:** Assign payoffs based on the quest's scoring rubric.

## Output
Judges must produce an Audit Report following the \`audit_report_template.md\`.
