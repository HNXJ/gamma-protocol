# GLOBAL_GAMMA: Project Doctrine

## The Trinity
- **Gamma** is the engine (Backend).
- **Gamma Protocol** is the law (Schemas/Contracts).
- **Gamma Arena** is the world (Frontend/UI).

## Core Vocabulary
- **Harnesses** are equipment (Capability boundaries).
- **Missions** are quests (Active objectives).
- **Receipts** are proof (Verifiable execution history).
- **Truth** is committed state (Committed backend substrate).
- **Observation** is everything shown (Visualizations/Reports).

## The Prime Mission: Continuous Growth
- **N → N+1**: Every step in Gamma aims to expand the validated neural substrate by exactly one biologically justified neuron.

## Roles
- **1 Judge**: Final arbiter of mission success and truth promotion.
- **4 Players**: Flexible identities (Builder, Tuner, Analyst, Tester) performing the expansion.

## Fundamental Laws
1. **Never confuse trial simulation with Judge validation.**
2. **Never confuse observation with truth.**
3. **Only Judge-validated PASS with a receipt can commit new truth.**
4. **Trial simulations are scratch artifacts only.**
