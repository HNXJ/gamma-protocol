# Hellenic Protocol Stack

The Hellenic stack provides a structured lifecycle for agentic scientific operations.

## BETA: Grounding
Read-only audit of the current state and source material. Establish the baseline for a new mission.

## ALPHA: Planning
Supervised decomposition of a mission into specific, bounded quests and patches.

## GAMMA: Execution
The exact execution of a quest within a harness-constrained environment. Produces receipts.

## DELTA: Reconciliation
Handling conflicts between proposed patches or divergent simulation results.

## THETA: Validation
Post-execution audit and autopsy to confirm that the execution artifact meets the mission's requirements and protocol standards.

## GAMMA-BURST: Refactor
Major doctrine shifts or repository-wide structural migrations.
