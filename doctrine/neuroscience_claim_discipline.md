# Neuroscience Claim Discipline

Gamma maintains strict discipline around neuroscience-related claims to avoid "hallucinated progress" and ensure biophysical grounding.

## Evidence Maps
- Literature-derived evidence maps are **model-rated projections**, not biological truth.
- They must record the source, the extraction agent, the judge, and the confidence score.

## Simulation and Modeling
- Simulation outputs are **execution artifacts**.
- A simulation "matching" data is a claim that requires THETA validation of the harness and tools used.

## Truth Safety
- Current active neuron counts or network states must not be asserted without a current, verified receipt from the truth plane.
- Null (no data) and Zero (measured absence) must be handled as distinct states in all evidence mappings.
